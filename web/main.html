Mushrooms can grow to be the size of a small car and can weigh over 20 pounds.

There are over 14,000 different species of mushrooms, with some bioluminescent and others psychedelic.

Some mushrooms are able to break down oil and other pollutants, making them useful for environmental cleanup.

Temperature

Different types of mushrooms grow best at different temperatures, but in general, they prefer a temperature range between 55째F and 75째F (12째C to 24째C).

Light

While mushrooms don't require light to grow, it can be used as a cue for their fruiting bodies to develop. Some growers use light cycles to help stimulate fruiting.

Oxygen

Mushrooms require oxygen to grow, but too much or too little can inhibit growth. Proper ventilation is important to ensure adequate oxygen levels.
